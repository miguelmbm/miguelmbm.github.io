The Foundation of Pet Takers

Pet takers Foundation was in 2020 during the rough era of many being in home due to the pandemic, the owner John Doe saw that many people were in need of pet care while they were at work or away from home, so he decided to create a company that takes care of pets while their owners are away, the company started small with only a few clients but as time went by the company grew and now it has many clients and is planning to expand in the future.

Fun fact about the owner of Pet Takers

Since when the owner was a child, he had to take care of 5 cats and 6 dogs.
